When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Tuesday, 15 March 2022, 16:00-18:00
Please be seated in the Chapel by 15:45 Ballroom Venue Chapel, Makiti Wedding Venue, Mulderdrift, Cradle of Human Kind

Nestled in a secluded valley under the Sterkfontein caves, lies a beautiful little chapel. At the Makiti Ballroom Venue, the happy couple will say their vows and start their lifelong journey. After the couple have said their vows (and you have showered them in flowers) you are invited to a cocktail hour, where the bride and groom capture their special day with friends and family.

Reception Party

Tuesday 15 March 2022; 18:00 – 22:00 Ballroom Venue Hall, Makiti Wedding Venue, Muldersdrift, Cradle of Humankind

Once all the photos are taken, we will move over to the Makiti Ballroom Venue’s reception hall where you can expect a buffet meal, and lots of cake! Please note that there will be a cash bar, and that no alcohol is allowed to be brought in for the ceremony or reception.

Gift Registry

Leo and Loreley will be starting a new life together and have made a list of items that will help them in their journey together. If you would like to give them a gift, please have a look at the wedding registry for things that they may like. You need not limit yourself to the items listed below, however, we ask that should you choose an item on the list that you "pledge" the item, this will inform other guests that you have already bought the gift and will prevent duplicates of some of the items. If you would prefer to give a gift card or cash donation to their future, we would like to inform you that there will be a safe at the gift table where you can safely leave your envelope to the happy couple.

Delivery

1. You can bring the gift to the wedding, neatly wrapped for the gift table.
2. Alternatively, you can click on the gift's name which will take you to an online website and have the gift delivered to Loreley’s parent's house in Randburg. Their address is “5A Dove Street, Ferndale, Randburg, 2194” and the contact number you can use is William Cairns 082 803 4657. Please specify that on delivery they should call, as there isn’t a bell at the front gate.
